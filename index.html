Smart Transportation

Smart transport uses technologies like IoT and AI to improve mobility.

Indicator: Proportion of population with convenient access to public transport.

  • Uses autonomous vehicles and mobility platforms.
  • Features AI-optimized traffic systems to reduce pollution and enhance accessibility.
  • Promotes low-emission solutions.

Target: 11.6 - Reduce the adverse per capita environmental impact of cities.

  • Efficient public and electric (EV) transportation systems lower urban pollution and carbon footprints.

Smart Waste Management

Smart waste management uses IoT and sensors to optimize waste collection, recycling, and disposal.

Target: 11.6 - Reduce environmental impact.

Indicator: Proportion of urban solid waste regularly collected and appropriately disposed of.

  • Enables real-time tracking of waste bins.
  • Optimizes waste collection routes.
  • Increases recycling rates, reducing landfill dependencies and environmental harm.

Target: 11.b - Integrating waste management with circular economy.

Indicator: Implementation of policies for climate-resilient development.
